Our Story

Founded in 2020, we started with a simple mission: to eliminate the clutter from your workspace.

What began as a small design studio in a garage has grown into a global brand trusted by creative professionals in over 50 countries. We believe that the objects you interact with every day should be beautiful, functional, and built to last.

Milestones

  • 2020: Launch of our first mechanical keyboard.
  • 2022: Expanded to audio and ergonomics.
  • 2024: Opened our first flagship store in Tokyo.
  • 2025: Launching the "Spring Collection" featuring sustainable materials.
